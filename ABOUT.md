# About Video extractor by ParallaXYZ

## 💡 Creation Story

This application was created to solve my personal tasks when working with video content.

### Why I Made This?

As a video maker, I constantly faced the need to:

1. **Download footage from YouTube** for use in my videos
   - Needed high-quality formats (720p50, 1080p50)
   - Important to have the ability to choose specific format
   - Required fast operation without unnecessary steps

2. **Convert video files** for compatibility with different projects
   - Often needed to re-encode to H.264 for editing
   - Required changing bitrate and quality
   - Wanted to have a tool at hand, not search for online services

### Why 2-in-1?

Instead of using different programs for downloading and conversion, I decided to combine both tools in one application. It's more convenient and faster:

- **Single interface** - no need to switch between programs
- **Common settings** - one folder for all files
- **Less resources** - one application instead of two

## 🎯 Main Idea

Video extractor by ParallaXYZ is not a commercial product, but a **personal tool** that I created for my needs and decided to share with others.

### Who is this application for?

- **Video makers** - downloading references and footage
- **Content creators** - working with video materials
- **Enthusiasts** - saving favorite videos in high quality
- **Anyone who needs a simple converter** - without complex settings

## 🛠️ Technical Decisions

### Why Edge, not Chrome?

Initially used Chrome, but encountered problems with cookie database locking. Edge proved to be a more stable solution for isolated profile.

### Why PyQt5?

- Cross-platform (although currently Windows only)
- Rich UI capabilities
- Good documentation
- Easy to create desktop applications

### Why yt-dlp?

- Most powerful tool for downloading from YouTube
- Regular updates
- Support for all formats
- YouTube restrictions bypass
